About Us
Businesses of all types understand the amount of time and resources it takes for Business Office Personnel to manage patient or consumer and self-pay accounts. This is where AutoPay of Montana steps in. We act as a professional extension of your business office to effectively and efficiently manage your Accounts Receivables and self-pay patient accounts.

Here is how it works:

A thorough review of your current practices and policies is undertaken with a Client Services Representative from AutoPay of Montana. Then, a custom program is designed to fit your needs, including consumer friendly contact procedures, monthly statement routines and follow-up calls and service to ensure professional, accurate and timely recovery of current account receivables. This program will be designed to increase recovery rates, handle your consumers in a professional and courteous manner while folding in all of your polices and procedures.
