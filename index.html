About Us
Businesses of all types understand the amount of time and resources it takes for Business Office Personnel to manage patient or consumer and self-pay accounts. This is where AutoPay of Montana steps in. We act as a professional extension of your business office to effectively and efficiently manage your Accounts Receivables and self-pay patient accounts.

Here is how it works:

A thorough review of your current practices and policies is undertaken with a Client Services Representative from AutoPay of Montana. Then, a custom program is designed to fit your needs, including consumer friendly contact procedures, monthly statement routines and follow-up calls and service to ensure professional, accurate and timely recovery of current account receivables. This program will be designed to increase recovery rates, handle your consumers in a professional and courteous manner while folding in all of your polices and procedures.

Welcome to AutoPay of Montana!

AutoPay of Montana is Montana’s premiere family owned billing company. We are here to assist consumers in achieving a successful end to their managed time-pay and self-pay accounts . Our reputation for integrity, confidentiality and performance has been earned with our consistent and professional service.

Designing a custom self-pay billing solution with AutoPay of Montana will also allow your staffing needs to be refocused, increase your cash flow and reduce the number of days your dollars are in A/R.


AutoPay of Montana has two locations to serve you and your consumers as well as a highly trained and professional staff. AutoPay of Montana staff is available for in-house training with your Business Office Personnel.



This benefit ensures that we are all on the same page and are working together to assist the consumer and clear your Accounts Receivables in a timely and profitable manner.
